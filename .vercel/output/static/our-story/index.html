Our Story

We grow together,
so our community
can rise together

Adriana's Foundation empowers Latino youth and women with the skills, mentorship, and opportunities needed to build meaningful careers and achieve economic mobility.

Through mentorship, life-skills training, certifications, and job placement, we prepare participants to succeed in today's workforce.

Our programs help participants move:
  • Uncertainty → Confidence
  • Low-wage work → Career pathways
  • Limited access → Real opportunity

Adriana
Gallardo

Access creates opportunity.
Opportunity creates leaders.

Adriana Gallardo's journey is one of resilience, faith, and purpose. Rising from nothing to becoming a successful entrepreneur, business coach, and international speaker — proud of her Latinx roots.

Adriana created Adriana's Foundation with a simple belief: true success is meant to be shared.

The Foundation exists to ensure that when one person rises, the community rises with them, so we can all grow together.

✦ Registered 501(c)(3) Nonprofit Organization
