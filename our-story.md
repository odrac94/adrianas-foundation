Our Story
Adriana’s Foundation empowers Latino youth and women with the skills, mentorship, and opportunities needed to build meaningful careers and achieve economic mobility.

Through mentorship, life-skills training, certifications, and job placement, we prepare participants to succeed in today’s workforce.

Our programs help participants move:

 From uncertainty to confidence
From low-wage work to career pathways
From limited access to real opportunity

We grow together, so our community can rise together.
